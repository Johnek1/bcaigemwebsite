About Us

About Us

We are the 2024 iGEM Team representing Bergen County Academies, a public magnet high school that offers students across Bergen County, New Jersey career-focused educational opportunities. The International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) Competition is a global synthetic biology competition. This year, the Bergen County Academies iGEM team is genetically modifying bacteria to fight cancer! Our team at BCA consists of students of diverse backgrounds and different focuses.

Founders: Muhammad El Sherbiny & Nicholas Ye
